# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Sports tournament organizers and managers who need to create, configure, and manage sports tournaments and leagues. They need a tool to manage brackets, registration, schedules, and scores efficiently.

## Product Purpose
To provide an all-in-one, comprehensive sports tournament management system. It aims to simplify the complex workflows of tournament structure setup, bracket generation, team registrations, match scoring, and public visibility.

## Brand Personality
Serious, Comprehensive, and Professional. The voice is precise, authoritative, and detail-oriented. The goal is to equip users with complete data integrity and elevate their professional standing as tournament administrators.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, dated web interfaces from the early 2000s that look like dense spreadsheets.
- Loud, hyper-saturated gaming themes with neon colors and heavy glow/gradient effects.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity Over Clutter**: Present complex configuration settings (like registration rules and bracket paths) incrementally, keeping pages readable and focused.
- **Confident Defaults**: Provide logical default values and clean templates for tournament formats so organizers can launch quickly without analysis paralysis.
- **High legibility on the field**: Ensure strong color contrast and clear typography so that indicators are easily readable outdoors on a court or field.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Adherence to WCAG AA color contrast standards, especially for active status states.
- High mobile responsiveness for managers updating scores on-site using phones or tablets.
